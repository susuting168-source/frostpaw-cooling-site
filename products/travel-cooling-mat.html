Travel Cooling Mat

Foldable Pet Cooling Pad for Car & Crate

The Travel Cooling Mat folds flat for easy packing and is designed for use in car trunks, pet crates, travel bags and outdoor shaded areas. It is a strong-selling SKU for pet travel brands, ecommerce sellers and subscription box buyers. Supports custom size, logo and compact retail packaging.

The Travel Cooling Mat is available as a base model for OEM and private-label programs. You can customize size, surface fabric, color, logo placement (woven label, printed logo, hang tag), retail packaging (poly bag, retail box, color sleeve) and carton marks for Amazon FBA or third-party warehouse delivery.

For wholesale buyers ordering without customization, we offer competitive per-unit pricing with low MOQ. Share your target quantity and market requirements — we will prepare a practical quotation and product proposal.
